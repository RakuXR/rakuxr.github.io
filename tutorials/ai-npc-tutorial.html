Creating Intelligent NPCs with Behavior Trees

Build intelligent NPCs with advanced AI-driven decision making, navigation, and voice interaction.

← All Tutorials

What This Tutorial Covers

This tutorial teaches you how to create intelligent NPCs with behavior trees and the Raku AI subsystem, including:

  • Behavior Trees — Modular, hierarchical decision-making with selectors, sequences, conditions, and decorators
  • Patrol AI — Waypoint-based movement for guard and enemy NPCs
  • Detection & Chase — Perception system with vision cones, hearing ranges, and line-of-sight
  • Blackboard State — Shared key-value store for NPC state management across the behavior tree
  • NavMesh Navigation — Pathfinding around obstacles, through doorways, and up stairs
  • Voice Commands — On-device voice recognition with SLM-powered dynamic dialogue (privacy-first, no cloud)
  • Complete Example — A fully functional guard NPC that patrols, detects, chases, and responds to voice
