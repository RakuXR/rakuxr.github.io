Company Overview

RakuAI is an AI-native spatial runtime that lets any AI assistant supercharge experiences in the real world — over a six-tool Model Context Protocol surface for world-model orchestration. The company began in 2025 as an MBA capstone project (originally under the BladeWireless name) and rebranded to RakuAI in February 2026. It is built on a patent estate with priority dating to July 2012 — five granted US patents covering AI game generation, dual-device AR interaction, and location-based experiences — IP that anchors today’s AR-glasses category.

Built for glasses, not laptops: 18ms motion-to-photon latency, sub-millimeter spatial precision, OpenXR conformant on Meta Quest 3, Xreal Air, Samsung Android XR, and MiRZA AR2. Multi-vendor by design — Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, and Copilot all drive the same runtime.

The platform serves five markets from 9,500+ REST API endpoints (across 17 subsystem routers) backed by 10,328 native engine functions: tourism AR (TimeLens), enterprise training (SafeSpace), fitness gaming (TrailQuest), developer tools (RakuForge API), and defense/public safety (TacView). The engine spans 18 native DLLs covering physics simulation, spatial audio, AI agent behavior trees, on-device ML, multiplayer networking, and GPS-anchored world persistence.

How It Works (High Level)

  1. Create a world anchored to GPS coordinates
  2. Ingest real-world 3D geometry from Google Maps
  3. Place interactive entities at real locations
  4. Add AI-powered NPCs with personality and memory
  5. Run physics, spatial audio, and multiplayer
  6. Players experience it through their phone's camera
