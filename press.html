Company Overview

RakuAI is an AI-native spatial game engine that generates interactive 3D experiences at real GPS coordinates from natural language. Founded in 2012, the company holds five granted US patents covering AI game generation, dual-device AR interaction, and location-based experiences.

The platform serves five markets from a single 21-endpoint API: tourism AR (TimeLens), enterprise training (SafeSpace), fitness gaming (TrailQuest), developer tools (RakuForge API), and defense/public safety (TacView). The engine includes 2,636 C API functions across 16 native DLLs, spatial audio, physics simulation, AI agent behavior trees, and GPS-anchored world persistence.

How It Works (High Level)

  1. Create a world anchored to GPS coordinates
  2. Ingest real-world 3D geometry from Google Maps
  3. Place interactive entities at real locations
  4. Add AI-powered NPCs with personality and memory
  5. Run physics, spatial audio, and multiplayer
  6. Players experience it through their phone's camera
