Frequently asked

Is Pro Beta really free right now?

Yes. Pro Beta is free while Raku Capture is in beta. We’ll email you at least 30 days before any charge begins, and you can stay on Free if you don’t want to upgrade. The expected price at general availability is $14.99/month.

What counts as a “capture”?

One uploaded scan that we reconstruct into a 3D scene. The day counter resets at midnight UTC. Re-opening or sharing an existing capture doesn’t count.

What happens to captures after the retention window?

On Free, captures and their reconstructed scenes are deleted 7 days after upload. On Pro Beta it’s 90 days. You can always re-upload the source video. Enterprise plans get custom retention.

Is MCP really included on Free?

Yes. The MCP runtime is production-deployed and available on every tier — Free included. Connect Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, or Copilot to your captures and they can read the scene over the Model Context Protocol.

When does GPU-accelerated reconstruction land?

Server-side splat training on NVIDIA A10G / L40S is on the roadmap via NVIDIA Inception cloud credits. Today reconstruction runs on CPU; once GPU lands we’ll route Pro Beta and Enterprise traffic to it first.

I want to ship this in our product / glasses / fleet.

That’s Enterprise. Email partners@rakuai.com with your use case and rough scale and we’ll get back to you.
