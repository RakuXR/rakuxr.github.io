Frequently Asked Questions

What happens when I hit my daily limit?

Free users get 10 generations per day. When you hit the limit, you'll see an option to upgrade to Pro for 100 generations/day, or wait until the next day. Your existing games remain playable.

What's the difference between Free and Pro AI?

Free uses our fast AI model for quick generation. Pro uses a higher-quality model that produces more creative, detailed game configurations with richer gameplay mechanics.

How does the free trial work?

Your 7-day Pro trial starts immediately with full access to all Pro features. You won't be charged until the trial ends. Cancel anytime during the trial and you won't be billed.

Is there an annual plan?

Yes! Toggle the "Annual" switch above to see yearly pricing at $149.99/year — that's 2 months free compared to monthly billing.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel from your account settings or the Stripe customer portal. Your Pro features remain active until the end of your billing period. No refund needed — just cancel.

What payment methods do you accept?

We use Stripe for secure payment processing. All major credit cards, debit cards, and Apple Pay / Google Pay are supported.

Can I sell games I create?

Yes. You own the games you create. Pro tier includes standalone export for commercial distribution on Steam, itch.io, or any platform.
