HANDS

Hand Tracking

High-precision hand tracking for natural controller-free interactions. The most intuitive way to interact in XR.

  • High-precision skeletal hand tracking
  • Built-in gesture recognition
  • Natural pinch and grab interactions
  • Customizable gesture definitions
Key benefit: Controller-free interaction that feels natural. Users interact with your app using their real hands.
EYES

Eye Tracking

Gaze-driven interaction for intuitive UI navigation and advanced rendering optimization.

  • Natural gaze-based selection and interaction
  • Built-in analytics for UX research
  • Rendering optimization powered by gaze data
  • Combined eye and hand input
Key benefit: Faster, more intuitive UI navigation. Significant GPU performance gains through smart rendering optimization.
HAPTICS

Haptics

Rich tactile feedback across controllers and hands. From simple vibrations to texture simulation and adaptive triggers.

  • Precise controller vibration control
  • Controller-free hand haptics
  • Adaptive trigger support
  • Library of preset haptic patterns
Key benefit: Immersion through touch. Players feel surfaces, impacts, and environmental cues without looking.
MR

Mixed Reality

Blend virtual content with the real world. Passthrough, scene understanding, plane detection, and portal cutouts.

  • Passthrough with blending modes
  • Scene understanding and surface detection
  • Automatic object placement
  • Environment mapping
Key benefit: Games that live in your room. Place virtual objects on real tables, open portals on real walls.
ANCHORS

Spatial Anchors

Persistent world-locked content. Place objects that stay where you put them, across sessions and across users.

  • Persistent anchors that survive app restarts
  • Cross-device anchor sharing
  • Multi-user spatial experiences
  • Complete anchor lifecycle management
Key benefit: Persistent mixed reality. A chess game on your coffee table is still there tomorrow.
