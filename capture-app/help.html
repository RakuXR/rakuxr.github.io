How scanning works

A scan moves through a few stages.

1
Walk the roomA short checklist guides you.
2
CaptureThe live camera grabs keyframes.
3
UploadKeyframes are sent to the service.
4
AnalyzingThe service builds the 3D result.
5
ExploreThe result opens in a 3D viewer.

Nothing is captured before you open the camera.

Camera permission troubleshooting

Raku Capture needs camera access to scan.

The permission prompt never appeared

  • Use a secure connection.
  • You may have dismissed it.

You tapped Block by mistake

Once blocked, the browser remembers it.

  • iOS Safari: Settings.
  • Android Chrome.
  • Desktop Chrome / Edge.

Camera in use or a black preview

  • Close other apps holding the camera.
  • Fully close and reopen the browser.
  • Check the PWA system settings entry.

It opens the front camera

Raku Capture asks for the rear camera.

Tips for a good scan

The reconstruction is only as good as the footage.

Do

  • Light the room evenly.
  • Move slowly and steadily.
  • Cover the space from many angles.
  • Keep overlap.
  • Get close to detail.
  • Look up and down.

Avoid

  • Glass, mirrors, shiny surfaces.
  • Fast motion and quick turns.
  • Blank walls.
  • Dim or flickering light.
  • Moving things mid-scan.

Want real-world measurements?

A plain scan has no built-in sense of scale.

If reconstruction fails

Sometimes a scan ends on the error screen.

First, just retry

Tap Try again on the error screen.

If it keeps failing

  • Re-scan with more coverage.
  • Re-scan more slowly.
  • Remove the hard surfaces.
  • Check your connection.
  • Start a fresh capture.

Honest note on failures

The reconstruction backend is currently simulated.

Connect the result to an AI assistant

Once a scan reaches the 3D viewer, hand it to an AI assistant.

The assistant does not eyeball the answer.

The short version

  1. Install the Raku MCP server.
  2. Paste one config block.
  3. Ask the assistant to load your space.

Full instructions live in the docs.

What you can ask

  • How big is this room?
  • What is the ceiling height?
  • Will a king bed fit along the longest wall?
  • Does a desk fit under the window?
  • How far apart are the two windows?
  • Show me the room without the partition.

Two honest caveats

  • The scene is currently simulated.
  • No scale, no measurements.

FAQ

Is my scan uploaded anywhere?

Nothing leaves your device until the upload step.

Why does the result look like a placeholder?

Because it is one.

How long should reconstruction take?

Usually a minute or two.

Can I scan outdoors or a very large space?

Capture works best on a single enclosed room.

Does this work on AR glasses?

Raku Capture is built as an installable web app.

Do I need a developer account to connect an AI assistant?

No account, but you do need to run the Raku MCP server locally.
