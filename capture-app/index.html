Early access

Scan your space

This is where the Raku Capture web app loads — point your phone camera at a room, walk it for about twenty seconds, and a 3D Gaussian splat of that space comes back.

The capture client is in active development. We are wiring the phone-capture flow into the live reconstruction pipeline, and we will not put it here until a scan reliably produces a splat you can actually use.

What happens when it ships:
  1. Open this page on a phone and grant camera access.
  2. Walk the camera slowly around the space you want to capture.
  3. The frames upload, reconstruct into a splat, and open in a viewer your AI assistant can read over MCP.

The MCP runtime your assistant drives is already live today. You can connect it to Claude and start reasoning over sample scenes right now.
