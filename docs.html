Why RakuAI?

Five granted US patents. Priority July 2012. Decade-old IP that anchors today’s AR-glasses category. The runtime that Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, and Copilot all drive — multi-vendor by design.

Built for glasses, not laptops

18ms motion-to-photon latency. Sub-millimeter spatial precision. OpenXR conformant on Meta Quest 3, Xreal Air, Samsung Android XR, and MiRZA AR2.

World-model orchestration

Six-tool MCP surface for world-model orchestration. Every frame, every NPC, every interaction routed through the AI of your choice. Unity’s MCP targets scene manipulation at design time — RakuAI’s MCP runs at runtime, every frame.

Industrial-grade surface

18 native DLLs. 17 router modules. 9,500+ REST endpoints. 10,328 bound engine functions. The boring numbers behind a fun-feeling runtime.

Architecture Overview

The engine is the deterministic authority. World models — video predictors, neural radiance fields, physics foundation models — are contributors that plug in over MCP adapters. None of them override the engine’s authority on what is actually happening in the simulation.
