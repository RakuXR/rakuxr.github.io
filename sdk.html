Why RakuAI?

5 granted US patents. Priority July 2012. Decade-old IP that anchors today’s AR-glasses category. 18 native DLLs. 17 router modules. 9,500+ REST endpoints. 10,328 bound engine functions. The runtime that Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, and Copilot all drive — multi-vendor by design.
