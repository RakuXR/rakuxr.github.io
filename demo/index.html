How This Works

This browser demo simulates the TimeLens Independence Hall AR experience that runs on RakuAI's spatial engine.

  • In the real experience: You walk through Independence Hall with a phone or AR headset. GPS waypoints trigger encounters with historical figures rendered in AR at their actual locations.
  • In this demo: The map shows the same waypoint layout, but you move a dot with your keyboard instead of walking. NPC encounters and stamp collection work the same way.
  • What partners see: The tour flow, dialogue system, stamp collection, and completion tracking that make up the visitor experience.
