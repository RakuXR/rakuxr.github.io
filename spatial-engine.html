PHYSICS

Physics Engine

Production-grade 3D physics simulation optimized for real-time XR experiences. Reliable, deterministic, and blazing fast.

  • Full rigid body simulation with multiple body types
  • Comprehensive collision detection and spatial queries
  • Advanced raycasting with layer-based filtering
  • Rich joint and constraint system
  • Fine-grained collision filtering for complex scenes
Key benefit: Deterministic physics simulation. Objects interact naturally in both VR and desktop modes.
VFX

Particle System

GPU-accelerated particle system for stunning visual effects at XR frame rates. Create cinematic VFX without compromising performance.

  • Massive-scale GPU-accelerated particle rendering
  • Multiple force field types for realistic simulations
  • Flexible emitter shapes for any effect
  • Beautiful particle trails with customizable appearance
  • Cascading effects with event-driven spawning
Key benefit: Cinematic VFX at XR frame rates. Fire, smoke, magic, rain, and explosions without frame drops.
ANIMATION

Animation System

Production-grade animation with advanced blending, state management, inverse kinematics, and cross-skeleton retargeting.

  • Advanced blend trees for smooth animation transitions
  • Powerful state machine system with flexible transitions
  • Industry-leading inverse kinematics for natural movement
  • Animation retargeting across different characters
  • Layered animation with masking and root motion
Key benefit: Animate any character naturally. IK makes hands reach doorknobs and feet match terrain automatically.
AUDIO

Spatial Audio

True 3D spatial audio that places sound in the world. Immersive, precise, and optimized for XR headsets.

  • Binaural 3D audio positioning for precise localization
  • Immersive surround sound rendering
  • Environmental reverb with automatic room adaptation
  • Realistic sound occlusion through walls and objects
  • Professional mixing and effects routing
Key benefit: Sound you can locate with your ears. Players hear enemies behind them, echoes in caves, muffled voices through doors.
